========================================================================
SUMMARY (plain language)
========================================================================

- We do NOT sell your data.
- We collect only what is needed to run the service.
- We share data only with analytics and error-tracking providers.
- We retain data for 365 days.
- You can export, correct, or delete your data at any time.
- Contact: privacy@ainative.studio

========================================================================
1. INFORMATION WE COLLECT
========================================================================

We collect the following categories of information:

  a) Account data: email address, name, authentication provider (GitHub OAuth
     or email/password), account creation date.

  b) Usage data: API requests, model calls, credit consumption, feature usage
     events, session metadata (IP address, user-agent, timestamps).

  c) Project data: files, embeddings, vector database rows, agent memory
     records, and other content you create within AINative Studio.

  d) Payment data: Stripe handles all payment processing. We store only
     transaction IDs, amounts, and subscription status — never card numbers.

  e) Technical data: error logs, performance metrics, crash reports (via
     Sentry or equivalent error-tracking service).

========================================================================
2. HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION
========================================================================

  - To provide, operate, and improve AINative Studio services.
  - To authenticate your identity and secure your account.
  - To calculate and bill API usage credits.
  - To send transactional emails (account confirmation, billing receipts).
  - To diagnose errors and improve platform reliability.
  - To generate aggregate, anonymized analytics (no individual profiling).

We do NOT use your data to train AI models without explicit opt-in consent.

========================================================================
3. DATA SHARING
========================================================================

We share data only with the following categories of third parties:

  - Analytics: Google Analytics 4, Google Tag Manager (aggregate usage stats)
  - Error tracking: Sentry or equivalent (anonymized crash data)
  - Payment processing: Stripe (payment data only; governed by Stripe's policy)
  - Infrastructure: Railway (hosting), Cloudflare (CDN, DDoS protection)

We do NOT sell, rent, or trade your personal data to any third party.

========================================================================
4. DATA RETENTION
========================================================================

  - Account data: retained for the life of your account + 30 days after
    deletion request.
  - Usage logs: 365 days rolling window.
  - Project data: retained until you delete it or close your account.
  - Backups: purged within 90 days of account deletion.

========================================================================
5. YOUR RIGHTS
========================================================================

You have the right to:

  - Access: request a copy of all data we hold about you.
  - Correction: update inaccurate personal data.
  - Deletion: request erasure of your account and associated data.
  - Export: download your project data in standard formats.
  - Objection: opt out of analytics tracking.

To exercise any right, email: privacy@ainative.studio
Response time: within 30 days.

========================================================================
6. SECURITY
========================================================================

  - All data in transit encrypted via TLS 1.3.
  - Data at rest encrypted via AES-256 (Railway managed).
  - API keys hashed before storage.
  - No plaintext passwords stored.
  - Security disclosures: https://ainative.studio/.well-known/security.txt

========================================================================
7. COOKIES
========================================================================

We use:
  - Session cookies: required for authentication (no opt-out possible).
  - Analytics cookies: Google Analytics 4 (opt-out via browser settings or
    uBlock Origin).
  - No third-party advertising or tracking cookies.

========================================================================
8. CHILDREN
========================================================================

AINative Studio is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly
collect data from children. Contact privacy@ainative.studio if you believe
a child has provided us data.
